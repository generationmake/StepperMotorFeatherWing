changes

version 1.0 - 2019-12-31
* initial release

version 1.1 - 2020-07-11
* added external supply for 5V logic of TMC5130
* added clock generator to have common clock for both motor drivers
* added LEDs on supply voltage and DIAG outputs of motor drivers
* added capacitors on current sense resistors
* added digital temperature sensor LM75 (U4)
* changed connector for encoder to JST SH
* added expansion connector CN2 with all unused pins
* changed position of mounting holes for heat spreader
* changed L1 and L2 to Sumida 0624CDMCCDS for better availability
